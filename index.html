Why this webpage ?

The objective is twofold:

  • from one side I track all my little discovers and tricks found around the web or books on how to let my system (or systems) properly working
  • from another side I think it may be helpfull if I share, so maybe someone else could take advantage

As said, whatever is reported in this webpage comes from searches around the web or from books. I will provide the sources and references as soon as those are known to me as well. For my part, there is absolutely no intention of appropriating other people's work.

My intention is to grown the webpage with a minimalistic approach using HTML 1.0 or 2.0, so it can be accessed not only by modern browsers but also from text-bases browser from DOS systems.
